About GBS Publishers and Distributors

GBS Publishers and Distributors (India) was established in 1991, and is now one of the leading publishers, distributors, and subscription agents of books/journals on all subjects, with its own premises for office, warehousing, and mailing. In addition to our own publications, GBS Publishers supplies publications from different Indian publishers on a wide variety of subjects to a large number of overseas buyers — trade, libraries, and individual scholars. We are the distributors of over 500 leading Indian Publishers. We have some of the finest Universities, Research Institutes, Government Libraries, Publishers, Distributors, Bookstores, and an ever-growing number of individuals around the world among our esteemed customers. We are a Member of the Exports Promotion Council of India (CAPEXIL).

Today GBS Publishers is confident that they are capable of procuring any title from India that probably no other Indian distributors can provide in a single stroke. GBS Publishers, through its hard work, has built a strong network over time to impart quality services again and again to its valued customers, who we take pride in associating with.

Publishing

Having long experience in the field of books/journals export, GBS Publishers now aims at publishing high-quality scholarly and research journals on engineering, computer sciences, science and technology, and related subjects. GBS Publishers publishes books and journals for the higher education, research, and professional markets mainly on engineering, computer sciences, science and technology, and related subjects. GBS Publishers welcome new proposals for books/journals in Pure and Applied Mathematics, Mechanics, and related areas. Authors are requested to send a brief synopsis of their work and a list of contents to the address given below. GBS provides publishing services for authors, scholarly and professional societies, and associations, including production, online delivery, sales, subscriptions, marketing, and customer services.
